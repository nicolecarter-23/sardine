How To Play

Click the sardine can to earn sardines.

Buy upgrades to earn more per click.

Buy cats to earn sardines automatically.

  • Lazy Cat: 1 sardine/s
  • Regular Cat: 5 sardines/s
  • Rich Cat: 15 sardines/s
  • Fat Cat: 25 sardines/s

Unlock achievements as you progress!

  • 100 Sardines
  • Official Cat Owner: Purchase your first cat
  • 1000 Sardines
  • Crazy Cat Lady: Purchase 10 Cats
  • Cat Cafe Licsense: Purchase 15 Cats
  • 100,000 Sardines
